Here's The Truth

The New Tax Laws Are Actually
More Favorable Than The Old Ones.

Yes, really. The Nigeria Tax Act 2025 (effective January 2026) raised the tax-free threshold from ₦300,000 to ₦800,000 per year.

If you earn ₦66,667 or less per month? You owe zero personal income tax.

The maximum tax rate (25%) is lower than South Africa (45%), Kenya (35%), and Egypt (27.5%).

There are legitimate deductions you can claim — rent, internet, software, equipment — that most people don't even know about.

The system isn't designed to crush you.
The problem is nobody explained it properly.
